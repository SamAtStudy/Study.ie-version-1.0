What is Study.ie?


Study is for learners as we reduce the amount of steps someone needs to take to find learning.

Search

To make the searching experience more appealing to our users, we went out and collected all of the most popular, recommended, and highly regarded courses from all over the internet. This can all be search from our top of the line search bar, on top of that if you have any course recommendations which you would like to pass please let us know.


Refine and Compare

We then want to further comfort our users by allowing them to personalise their search and results through price and the location in which they want to learn if it an offline course. Course reviews are then the cherry on top, as you get to decide what's right for you based on what you can compare.


Book

In order to begin on your newly encountered learning path, simply book the course and be on your way. You may then return to our website to track your learning on your Study account.

Who Are We?

Our Mission

Empower any learner* to effortlessly find the best resources and help them visualise their learning journey.

*If you can think, you are a learner.

About Study

We believe in the learner's natural need for the best learning.

We quench this thirst by having all the best resources in one searchable location

We then help them track not only what they've done in the past and present, but more importantly where their learning can advance to in the future.

Our Team

A small bunch on college students who are determined to help individuals see the value in what they're learning, and how their learning can take them to their deepest goals. You could call us the 'Avengers of Learning', or more realistically just your regular local boyband.
