How does study help me?


If you're someone who loves efficiency and finding the best things, we help you find learning resources that suit you the most.

Why should you search on study.ie?

Our team hates getting ads, irrelevant searches or even poor recommendations when we search for stuff. So we've gone and removed the chance of this by only having the best and most recommended resources to enhance your search for learning.


How can I recommend resources I have found useful?

By recommending that one golden piece of learning you have encountered that changed your life and learning route, you are essentialy helping others in tough similar situations. We all ger stuck and need guidance sometimes, together we can fill in these potholes.
